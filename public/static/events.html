CLUEMINATI

Guess if you can!

Event Description:

Show the Detective within you, fasten your seat belt and get ready for a fun event. Remember the Chinese proverb: "One picture is worth ten thousand words". So use your clever mind in finding a hidden message in every image. Collect as many coins as you can and be a Numismatist.

Rules:

1) The image itself will tell you a lot about the answer, you can take the help of the internet. If you still can't figure out the answer you can go for the clues.
2) Every player will carry 1000 coins at the beginning of the game.
3) There will be 3 clues for each challenge, unlocking the 1st clue will cost you 10 coins, 2nd clue will cost 15 coins, and 3rd will cost 20 coins.
4) To win this event, you have to be very fast; whoever submits the answer first will get 200 coins, subsequent particpants will get 5 coins lesser than the previous participant.
If you submit after 30 correct submissions by other participants, you will get 50 coins only
5) Use only lowercase alphabets, for eg. Type "A.P.J Abdul Kalam" as "apjabdulkalam" (without quotes)

CODE WARS

Use what you have learnt!

Event Description:

“Practice makes a man Perfect“. Competitive coding is a way to improve your coding skills. Compete with your peers in this 2 hour long coding challenge. The candidate with highest number of points will win. Any kind of plagiarism will lead you to get disqualified from all the events.

PLATFORM: HackerEarth

Rules:

1) Once you start the event, you won't be able to change the tabs.
2) You won't be allowed to copy/paste the code.
3) Your cameras will be turned on for complete 60 mins until you finish the event.
4) If any of these rules are manipulated, the participant won't be considered for any of the events.
